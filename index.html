A Green future

The UAE is leading in sustainability initiatives, launching a national climate change strategy to reduce carbon emissions, increase energy efficiency, and invest in renewable energy. The country has signed numerous international agreements and treaties to promote sustainable development. Innovative sustainable developments in the UAE are helping the country reduce its carbon footprint and become a pioneer in the region, demonstrating the importance of environmental conservation and climate change mitigation.

Emirates EcoAlliance is an ecological association affiliated with the Ministry of Climate Change and Environment in the UAE. We are a community united by a shared commitment to the environment and a burning desire to make a positive impact. As stewards of our country, we believe in the power of collective action to create lasting change.

GREEN INITIATIVES


UAE Net Zero 2050

The UAE Net Zero by 2050 strategic initiative is a national drive to achieve net-zero emissions by 2050, making the Emirates the first Middle East and North Africa (MENA) nation to do so. The initiative aligns with the Paris Agreement, which calls on countries to prepare long-term strategies to reduce greenhouse gas (GHG) emissions and limit the rise in global temperature to 1.5 C compared to pre-industrial levels.

Green Agenda 2030

The UAE’s Green Agenda 2030 was launched with an aim to increase the UAE’s GDP by 4 to 5 percent, increase its exports by about AED 24 to 25 billion and reduce the country’s emissions to less than 100 kilowatt-hours. The UAE’s Green Agenda - 2030 is a long-term plan to achieve the goals of sustainable development in the UAE and make its economy more environment-friendly. It has strategic objectives and includes various programmes and initiatives to achieve them.

Masdar City

Masdar City is an urban community in Abu Dhabi, the capital of the United Arab Emirates. Initiated in 2008, Masdar City is Abu Dhabi's pioneering vision of a city of the future, with the goal of it becoming the world's most sustainable eco-city. Driven by the UAE’s commitment to sustainability, Masdar City is driving global solutions to climate change and helping create cities of the future.

UAE Agenda 2030

Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) also known as Global Goals are a set of 17 goals that aim to provide better living conditions to all. The SDGs are based on the United Nations’ Millennium Development Goals. The SDGs are part of 2030 Agenda for Sustainable Development.
